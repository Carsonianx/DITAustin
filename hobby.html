My Hobbies

A few hobbies of mine include sports, instruments, and playing video games. I started playing inline hockey at the age of 6 and competitively played and placed 1’st nationally out of NZ three times throughout my time playing inline hockey. Despite this I have decided to quit inline hockey this year after 8 years of dedication to the sport. The sport I play the most now is tennis, I have been playing for around 5 years. Another sport I play is table tennis as a hobby and not competitively. I placed 1st in primary and 3rd in intermediate for table tennis. Lastly I also have competed in badminton tournaments in the school team. I’ve also been playing piano for several years and have recently started learning guitar as a hobby. During my spare time I enjoy playing games and going out with my friends.
